Our Technology

Security

Everything we do is designed to give you control over your information. All the charging requests you send are encrypted. Charging data is stored on your device, never on our servers or in the "Cloud". WifiPower doesn’t share your credit card number. And the list goes on.

Fast Charging

The show must go on. WifiPower employs a new wireless frequency to charge your phone, allowing you to watch up to 13 hours of video on a single charge. When you do need to power up, just set it down on with our page open. We use a P2P seeding network to ensure you get the optimum charge going to 50 percent in 30 minutes.

Open Technologies

WifiPower is updated to run the latest features — and even researches those that haven’t been invented yet. So you can use our fast charging technology now. And take advantage of feature and security updates in the future. Our code is available to all, as it should be because we practice the honest policies of the open web.
